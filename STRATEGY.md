## Executive Summary

**The Opportunity:** Universities now REQUIRE AI disclosure. The market has multiple tools, but none offer a complete student-focused bundle.

**The Positioning:** "The only all-in-one ethical AI toolkit for students" - NOT a Turnitin bypass tool.

**The Moat:** Bundle value (5 tools in 1) + unique prompt coaching + student-first pricing ($5/mo).

**The Cost:** $0/month until 10K users using free-tier architecture.

## Market Validation (December 2025)

### The Problem is REAL and GROWING

| Statistic | Value | Trend |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Students using AI | 92% | Up from 66% (2024) |
| Students using AI for tests | 88% | Up from 53% (2024) |
| Students who FAIL to disclose | 74% | Significant gap |
| AI-generated citations that are fake | 47% | Major risk |

**Source:** HEPI 2025 Survey, King's Business School Study

### Why Students Don't Disclose

> "Mandatory disclosure statements feel like a confession or admission of guilt"

**Root Causes:**
- Disclosure feels like admitting wrongdoing
- No easy tool to generate statements
- Uncertainty about what to include
- Fear of being flagged by AI detection

## Unique Value Propositions

### 1. AI Disclosure Statement Generator

**What:** Auto-generate professional disclosure statements
**Format:** APA, MLA, Chicago, Generic
**Competition:** Grammarly Authorship, GAIDeT, Paperpal (see competitive analysis)
**Our Edge:** Works with ANY AI tool, not locked to one ecosystem

### 2. AI Prompt Coach (PRIMARY DIFFERENTIATOR)

**What:** Score prompts 0-100, suggest improvements
**Unique:** Student-focused, educational framing
**Competition:** Minimal - no direct student-focused prompt coaching tool exists
**Our Edge:** This is our strongest unique feature

### 3. AI Citation Verifier - PREMIUM (PLANNED)

**What:** Verify if AI-generated citations are real
**Why:** 47% of AI citations are fake/wrong
**Competition:** GPTZero Source Finder, Citea, SwanRef, Citely
**Our Edge:** Integrated with disclosure generation

### 4. Research with Real Sources

**What:** Search 200M+ academic papers
**APIs:** Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex (FREE)
**Competition:** Elicit ($12/mo), Consensus
**Our Edge:** Bundled with other tools at lower price

### 5. Free Grammar Check

**What:** LanguageTool integration
**Why:** Hook for free tier, not our focus
